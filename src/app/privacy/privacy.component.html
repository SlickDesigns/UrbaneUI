On our Public Website, we do not collect personal data, unless you provide your personal data through a contact form, a live chat, subscribe to the blog newsletter, or download our resources. Personal data collected on the Public Website is only used for the purposes for which it was provided. The following is a complete list of our Public Website’s purposes of data collection:


  1. Contact Form

    If you fill out our contact form, we will use the provided data to contact you to answer your question.

  2. Live chat

    If you contact us via a live chat, we will only use the provided data to contact you to answer your question.

  3. Newsletter Subscription

    If you subscribe to our newsletter, we will only use the collected email address in order to send you the newsletter until you unsubscribe from this service.

  4. Resource Consumption

    If you consume one of our most recent e-books or icon sets, we use the provided email address if and only if we believe there is a potential business opportunity, in which case our sales team might get in contact.

  5. Job Application

    If you apply for a job at Agente, we will only use your contact details provided for recruitment proposes.

We will retain collected personal data for as long as is necessary to provide you with the requested service unless a longer retention period is required by law.


We take adequate measures to protect your data from being stolen, lost, or used in any way that is not in line with the purposes for which the data was collected.


We do not sell your personal data to third parties.


We will not give your personal data to third parties, unless we are obligated by law, for example in case of a lawful request by law enforcement authorities
